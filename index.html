Games
Eyes of Minerva

Stealth first person puzzle game, where the player will traverse a maze of rooms riddled with enemies. The player will have to use all the tools at their disposal to either distract or disable the enemies.

ToyTank!

3D action platformer where you have to go from point A to point B using your tank skills... You must go through all the different levels and escape from the room.

Gooh

2D platformer where the player has a gun that shoots jelly, which allows them to jump and reach higher places. They will have to fight enemies and avoid hazzards in order to escape.

My City Life

My City Life is a project where I combine Finite State Machines to have people with different states (the citizens of the city), and pathfiniding so they move correctly across the city, not crossing the street for example.

Real-Time Pencil Rendering

Final project for my CS562: Advanced Real-Time Rendering Techniques class. The project consists on rendering 3D objects in pencil style drawing. This tries to simulate the human way of drawing things.

Projects

My City Life

Personal project made with a custom engine that I did using C++. It shows a simulation of a small city in which people has different activities to do (for example, go to the cinema when they are bored, go to a restaurant when they are hungry, go home when they are tired, ...). The entities use pathfinding to move around the city. The logic to transition from one activity to another is done with Finite State Machines.

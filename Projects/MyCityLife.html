My City Life

[Work In Progress]

About

Team: Individual project

Tecnhology: Custom C++ engine made from scratch

High concept : My City Life is a project where I combine Finite State Machines to have people with different states (the citizens of the city), and pathfiniding so they move correctly across the city, not crossing the street for example.


The city

Citizens

They move around the city going to specific places depending on their states:

  • Hungry
  • Energy
  • Exercise
  • Fun


House

People go there to rest. It increase the citizen's energy bar.




Gym

People go there to exercise. It increase the citizen's exercise bar.




Restaurant

People go there to eat. It decreases the citizen's hungry bar.




Park

People go there to have fun. It is one of the places inside the entertainment category. It increases the citizen's fun bar.




Cinema

People go there to have fun. It is one of the places inside the entertainment category. It increases the citizen's fun bar.




Road

People can't cross the road, so in order to go to a place that is on the other side of the road, they will have to find a way around it.

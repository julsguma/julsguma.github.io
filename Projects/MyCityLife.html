My City Life

Team

Individual project.

Tecnhology

Custom C++ engine made from scratch.

High concept

Personal project that shows a simulation of a small city in which people has different activities to do (for example, go to the cinema when they are bored, go to a restaurant when they are hungry, go home when they are tired, ...). The entities use pathfinding to move around the city. The logic to transition from one activity to another is done with Finite State Machines.

The city

Citizens

They move around the city going to specific places depending on their states:

  • Hungry
  • Energy
  • Exercise
  • Fun
House

People go there to rest. It increase the citizen's energy bar.

Gym

People go there to exercise. It increase the citizen's exercise bar.

Restaurant

People go there to eat. It decreases the citizen's hungry bar.

Park

People go there to have fun. It is one of the places inside the entertainment category. It increases the citizen's fun bar.

Cinema

People go there to have fun. It is one of the places inside the entertainment category. It increases the citizen's fun bar.

Road

People can't cross the road, so in order to go to a place that is on the other side of the road, they will have to find a way around it.
